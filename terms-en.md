# Terms of Use

The Alexa Skill **KARA – Adhan (Ezan) Assistant** provides prayer times, reminders, and religious content.

## Purpose

The skill provides prayer times, prayer reminders (optionally including the Adhan), and short religious reminders (e.g. Hadith) for informational and inspirational purposes.

No guarantee is given regarding accuracy, completeness, or availability of the content.

The functionality of the skill may be modified or extended at any time.
